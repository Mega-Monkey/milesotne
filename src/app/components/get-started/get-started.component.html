So you have seen what a typical contract flow looks like when using MilestonePay, let's take a deeper look at what service providers and clients can look forward to.

Service Providers

Clarity
Clearly defined deliverables and acceptance criteria

Every Milestone that you work on will have work items defined with your client upfront, in black and white, written by you and agreed upon by both parties.

Reduce Project Overhead
Automatic Project Management Updates

As you complete tasks your team and client are made completely aware of how you are tracking. This reduces the time you need to spend in meetings and increases the time you can dedicate to your craft.

Get Paid On Time
Your clients' payments made on your terms

You can decide if a Milestone will be paid upfront, paid when you finish or use the preferred hybrid MilestonePay (funds are taken upfront and held securely, then released once work is complete).

Clients

Peace of Mind
Your money is protected until the work is complete

Any agreed deliverables must meet your previously agreed acceptance criteria in order for MilestonePay to pay your service provider. If your service provider elects not to complete the work or both parties agree to terminate the contract the customer is refunded any outstanding credit.

Calls not Required
Service Provider Status Reports

You can log in at any time and see how your work is progressing and also be notified by email. This saves you the hassle of taking the time out of your busy day to do quick check-ins.

Change Requests
You want to change a deliverable

You can see your service provider is working well and completing your tasks. However you notice a piece of the project you would love to change that wasn't previously agreed to in your contract. Our change request flow has you covered. Request the change and let your service provider alter the price and delivery timeline to accomodate your new request.
